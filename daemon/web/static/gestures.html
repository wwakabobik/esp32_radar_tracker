Near zone

Distance band (cm) where the device listens for gestures. Too narrow or far from your usual hand position → misses; too wide → false triggers.

Hold — how long the hand must stay in the near zone before an action fires. Debounce — minimum gap between consecutive next/prev commands (device + Mac).

Zone hold → next track

Simple rule: keep your hand in the near zone for Hold ms → skip to next track. After firing, remove your hand from the zone (Armed: no — remove hand) before you can trigger again. Works without ML; recommended as the default.

ML gestures (TinyML on device)

Optional classifiers trained on radar patterns — swipe/hover motions rather than plain distance hold. Requires AI enabled in Settings. Each action can be toggled independently. Keep Require near zone on unless you know what you are doing.

Volume range — when hover is detected, hand distance between Min and Max maps to 0–100% volume. Volume interval — minimum time between volume updates.
